From empathy to ensemble.

I’m Aurora — someone who listens before plays.

Music, to me, is a way of noticing: the quiet between notes, the gestures that hold a crowd together.
I drift between sound and silence, between rhythm and reflection, searching for the moment when empathy turns into harmony.

This space is where those fragments gather — the stages I’ve built, the songs that shaped me, the people who taught me to hear.
